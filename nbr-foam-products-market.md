<p><ul><li>Closed Cell Rubber</li><li>Semi-closed Cell Rubber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>NBR foam products are classified into closed cell rubber and semi-closed cell rubber types. Closed cell rubber features tightly sealed cells, offering excellent resistance to water, oils, and chemicals, making it ideal for applications requiring high durability and insulation. In contrast, semi-closed cell rubber has a mix of sealed and open cells, providing moderate resistance to moisture while allowing for greater flexibility and compressibility. Both types find applications in automotive, construction, and industrial sectors due to their versatile performance characteristics.</p></p>
