<p><p>The 3D interposer market consists of three main types: silicon, organic, and glass interposers. Silicon interposers offer high performance with excellent electrical properties, making them ideal for advanced applications. Organic interposers are lightweight and cost-effective, suitable for consumer electronics and are often used for their flexibility. Glass interposers, known for their high thermal stability and low signal loss, are gaining traction in high-frequency applications. Together, these types address diverse needs in modern semiconductor packaging and system integration.</p></p>
