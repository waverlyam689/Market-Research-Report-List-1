<p><p>Fiber Optic Testing Equipment (FOTE) encompasses various tools essential for assessing the performance and integrity of fiber optic networks. Key types include Optical Light Sources (OLS) for generating light signals, Optical Power Meters (OPM) for measuring signal strength, and Optical Loss Test Sets (OLTS) for assessing overall link loss with precision. Remote Fiber Test Systems (RFTS) enable remote diagnostics and monitoring, while Optical Spectrum Analyzers (OSA) provide detailed analysis of wavelength and signal quality, ensuring efficient network functioning.</p></p>
