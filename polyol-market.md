<p><strong>The Polyol Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyester Polyol</li><li>Polyether Polyol</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyol market is primarily divided into two types: polyester polyols and polyether polyols. Polyester polyols are derived from adipic acid and are commonly used in the production of polyurethane foams, offering improved thermal stability and mechanical properties. In contrast, polyether polyols are produced through the polymerization of oxides like ethylene and propylene, providing flexibility and water resistance, making them ideal for applications in flexible foams and coatings. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including automotive and construction.</p></p>
