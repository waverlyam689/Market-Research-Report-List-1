<p><p>The **Automotive Storage Battery Market** includes **Traditional Lead-acid Storage Batteries**, known for their affordability and reliability, using lead plates and sulfuric acid electrolyte. They require periodic maintenance, like water topping. In contrast, **Maintenance-Free Storage Batteries** are sealed, eliminating the need for water refills, offering convenience and reduced upkeep. Both types power vehicles, but maintenance-free batteries are increasingly preferred for their ease of use, durability, and lower maintenance costs, aligning with modern automotive demands for efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
