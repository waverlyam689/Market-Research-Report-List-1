<p><p>The medical breathable PE tape market is primarily categorized into two types: Low-Density Polyethylene (LDPE) and High-Density Polyethylene (HDPE). LDPE is known for its superior flexibility and softness, making it ideal for use on sensitive skin and for applications requiring conformability. In contrast, HDPE offers enhanced strength and durability, suitable for more robust medical applications. Both materials provide breathable properties, ensuring moisture management and comfort for patients while securing dressings and devices effectively.</p></p>
