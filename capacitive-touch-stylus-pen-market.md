<p><p>The capacitive touch stylus pen market is divided into two main types: active and passive capacitive touch stylus pens. Active capacitive stylus pens feature built-in electronics that enable pressure sensitivity, palm rejection, and programmable buttons, making them ideal for precision tasks, drawing, and note-taking. In contrast, passive capacitive stylus pens rely on a simple conductive tip, offering basic functionality for navigation and touch input without advanced features, making them more affordable and suited for casual use on capacitive screens.</p></p>
