<p><strong>The Gastrointestinal Stents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-expanding Metal Stents</li><li>Plastic Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gastrointestinal stents market is primarily categorized into self-expanding metal stents and plastic stents. Self-expanding metal stents are designed to provide long-term support in obstructed passages, ensuring patency and reducing complications. They are typically used in conditions like esophageal or biliary obstructions. In contrast, plastic stents are often used for temporary solutions, being more cost-effective but less durable. They are commonly employed in managing bile duct obstructions or gastrointestinal leaks, offering flexibility in treatment options.</p></p>
