<p><p>The 3D Hot Bending Glass Graphite Mold market is categorized into three main types: Solid Mold, Hollow Mold, and Frame Mold. Solid Molds provide robust support for forming intricate glass shapes, ensuring uniform heat distribution. Hollow Molds, often used for creating complex, lightweight structures, allow for internal cavities that reduce material use. Frame Molds serve as supportive structures that guide the glass during bending, maintaining precise dimensions and enhancing stability throughout the heating process. Together, these molds facilitate diverse glass design applications.</p></p>
