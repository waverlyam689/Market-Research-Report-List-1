<p><p>The flow cytometry buffer market encompasses various types, each catering to specific applications. Surface staining buffers facilitate the labeling of cell surface markers, enabling the identification and characterization of different cell populations. Fixation buffers preserve cellular structures and maintain marker integrity for accurate analysis. Viability staining buffers differentiate live and dead cells, crucial for assessing cell health. Other buffers include those for intracellular staining and washing, supporting broader immunophenotyping and functional studies in research and clinical diagnostics.</p></p>
