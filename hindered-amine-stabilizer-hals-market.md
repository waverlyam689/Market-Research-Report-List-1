<p><p>Hindered Amine Stabilizers (HALS) are essential additives used to enhance the longevity and thermal stability of polymers. The market can be segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Polymeric Type**: These stabilizers are large, polymer-like structures offering excellent performance in high molecular weight applications. </p><p>2. **Monomeric Type**: Smaller and simpler in structure, monomeric HALS provide effective stabilization but typically have limited durability. </p><p>3. **Oligomeric Type**: These intermediates possess characteristics of both polymeric and monomeric HALS, balancing performance and cost-effectiveness for varied applications.</p></p>
