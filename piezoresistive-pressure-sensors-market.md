<p><ul><li>Diffuse Silicon Piezoresistive</li><li>Ceramic Piezoresistive</li><li>Strain Gage Piezoresistive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezoresistive pressure sensors market includes three main types: diffuse silicon piezoresistive sensors, ceramic piezoresistive sensors, and strain gauge piezoresistive sensors. Diffuse silicon sensors offer high accuracy and temperature stability, ideal for applications in automotive and industrial sectors. Ceramic sensors are known for their robustness and resistance to harsh environments, making them suitable for medical and aerospace applications. Strain gauge sensors provide flexibility in design and are widely used in various pressure measurement applications due to their cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
