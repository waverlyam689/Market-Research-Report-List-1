<p><p>The CMP consumables market primarily encompasses two key components: CMP slurries and CMP pads. CMP slurries are abrasive mixtures used in chemical mechanical polishing processes to smooth and planarize semiconductor surfaces, providing essential precision in chip fabrication. CMP pads, on the other hand, serve as the mechanical interface between the wafer and the slurry, aiding in the effective application of pressure and facilitating the polishing action. Together, they are critical for achieving optimal performance and quality in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
