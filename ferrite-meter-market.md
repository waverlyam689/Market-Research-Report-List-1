Area probes are designed to assess the ferrite content in larger surface areas, making them ideal for applications in coatings and welded joints. In contrast, diameter probes are specifically engineered for measuring ferrite levels in cylindrical materials or pipes, ensuring precise readings in confined spaces. Both types play a crucial role in quality control and material testing across industries like manufacturing and construction, ensuring compliance with industry standards.</p></p>
