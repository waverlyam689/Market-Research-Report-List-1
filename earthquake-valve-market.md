<p><ul><li>Motion Sensing Caged Ball</li><li>Excessive Flow Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The earthquake valve market comprises primarily two types: motion sensing caged ball valves and excessive flow sensor valves. Motion sensing caged ball valves detect ground movement and automatically close to prevent hazardous fluid release during an earthquake. Excessive flow sensor valves monitor fluid flow rates and shut off when abnormal levels are detected, indicating potential pipe damage or rupture. Both types enhance safety in critical infrastructures by mitigating risks associated with seismic activity, ensuring swift and automatic responses to minimize damage.</p></p>
