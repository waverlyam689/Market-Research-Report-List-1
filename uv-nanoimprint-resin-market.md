<p><strong>The UV Nanoimprint Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Permanent Layer</li><li>Sacrificial Layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV Nanoimprint Resin market is segmented into two main types: Permanent Layer and Sacrificial Layer. Permanent Layer resins are designed to form durable, long-lasting patterns that remain on the substrate, crucial for applications in electronics and optics. In contrast, Sacrificial Layer resins are temporarily applied and later removed, allowing for later processing steps without affecting the underlying structures. Both types are vital for enhancing manufacturing precision in nanostructures and improving the performance of devices.</p></p>
