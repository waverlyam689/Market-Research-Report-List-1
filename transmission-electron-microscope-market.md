<p><ul><li>Conventional Transmission Electron Microscope</li><li>Low-Voltage Electron Microscope</li><li>Cryo-Microscopy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Transmission Electron Microscope (TEM) market comprises three main types: Conventional Transmission Electron Microscopes, which provide high-resolution imaging of specimen at atomic levels; Low-Voltage Electron Microscopes, designed to minimize specimen damage by operating at lower voltages while still delivering detailed images; and Cryo-Microscopy, which preserves samples at cryogenic temperatures, allowing for the visualization of biological specimens in near-native states. These technologies cater to various applications across materials science, biology, and nanotechnology, enhancing research and diagnostic capabilities.</p></p>
