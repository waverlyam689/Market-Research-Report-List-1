<p><ul><li>Wet</li><li>Dry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vibratory polisher market includes two primary types: wet and dry. Wet vibratory polishers utilize water or liquid additives to enhance the polishing process, effectively reducing dust and facilitating smoother finishes. They are ideal for polishing delicate materials and intricate shapes. Conversely, dry vibratory polishers operate without liquids, relying on abrasive media to achieve the desired surface finish. This type is often used for more robust materials and applications where moisture could be detrimental. Each type serves distinct needs in various industries.</p></p>
