<p><p>The mobile grain dryers market consists of three main types: mounted, self-propelled, and trailed. Mounted dryers are attached to the back of tractors, offering flexibility and ease of use in various locations. Self-propelled dryers are standalone units with their own engines, enabling independent operation and mobility across fields. Trailed dryers are towed by tractors, allowing larger capacities while maintaining transportability. Each type caters to different operational needs, enhancing grain drying efficiency and convenience for farmers.</p></p>
