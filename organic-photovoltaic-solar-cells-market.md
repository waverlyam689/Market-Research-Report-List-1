<p><p>The organic photovoltaic solar cells market includes various types, notably single-layer and bilayer configurations. Single-layer organic photovoltaic cells consist of a single organic material layer that absorbs photons and generates electrical current, offering simplicity and ease of production. In contrast, bilayer organic photovoltaic cells feature two distinct layers of organic materials, typically a donor and an acceptor, enhancing light absorption and charge separation, which improves efficiency. Both types cater to different applications, balancing cost, performance, and scalability.</p></p>
