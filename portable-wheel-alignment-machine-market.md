<p><p>The portable wheel alignment machine market features two main types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and 3D wheel alignment machines. CCD wheel alignment machines use cameras to measure the angles of wheels through reflective targets, providing accurate alignment diagnostics. In contrast, 3D wheel alignment machines leverage advanced laser technology to capture wheel angles in real-time, offering faster and highly precise results. Both types enhance efficiency in vehicle maintenance, appealing to automotive service centers and workshops seeking reliable, mobile alignment solutions.</p></p>
