<p><ul><li>Air Horn</li><li>Electric Horn</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive loudspeaker market primarily consists of air horns and electric horns. Air horns utilize compressed air to produce a loud and powerful sound, commonly used in larger vehicles for signaling and alerts. They are characterized by their robust sound output and are often employed in emergency vehicles. Electric horns, on the other hand, rely on an electric current to generate sound, offering a range of tones and volumes. These are prevalent in passenger vehicles, providing a more standardized warning signal.</p></p>
