<p><strong>The OLED Mobilephone Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PMOLED</li><li>AMOLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The OLED mobile phone display market comprises two main types: PMOLED (Passive Matrix OLED) and AMOLED (Active Matrix OLED). PMOLED features a simpler, less expensive design, ideal for smaller screens and static displays, but offers lower resolution and refresh rates. In contrast, AMOLED utilizes an active matrix approach, allowing for higher resolutions, faster refresh rates, and better power efficiency, making it suitable for larger screens and dynamic content. This distinction drives their applications across various mobile devices.</p></p>
