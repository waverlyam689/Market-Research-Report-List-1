<p><strong>The High-end Copper Foil Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolled Copper Foil</li><li>Electrolytic Copper Foil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-end copper foil market comprises primarily two types: rolled copper foil and electrolytic copper foil. Rolled copper foil is produced through a mechanical process, offering superior thickness control and excellent surface finish, making it ideal for high-frequency applications. In contrast, electrolytic copper foil is manufactured through an electrochemical deposition process, resulting in a finer grain structure and enhanced conductivity, suitable for advanced electronics like PCBs and batteries. Each type caters to specific industry needs, balancing performance and application requirements.</p></p>
