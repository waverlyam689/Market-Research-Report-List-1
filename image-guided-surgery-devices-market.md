<p><p>The Image Guided Surgery Devices market comprises various imaging modalities that enhance surgical precision. X-Ray Fluoroscopy enables real-time imaging during procedures. Endoscopes allow for minimally invasive internal examinations. Position Emission Tomography (PET) is used for metabolic assessments, while Single Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) offers functional imaging, helping surgeons navigate complex anatomical structures during operations.</p></p>
