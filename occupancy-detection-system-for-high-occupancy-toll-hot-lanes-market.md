<p><p>Occupancy Detection Systems for High-Occupancy Toll (HOT) lanes are categorized into two market types: passive and active. Passive systems utilize existing traffic flow data to infer occupancy levels, relying on sensor technology like cameras or inductive loops to monitor vehicle counts and classifications. In contrast, active systems involve direct engagement with vehicles, employing technologies such as RFID or transponders to actively monitor occupancy in real-time. Both types aim to optimize traffic management and implement dynamic toll pricing based on lane usage.</p></p>
