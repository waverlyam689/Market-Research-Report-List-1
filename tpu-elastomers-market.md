<p><strong>The TPU Elastomers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyester-Based Type</li><li>Polyether-Based Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The TPU elastomers market is primarily divided into two types: polyester-based and polyether-based. Polyester-based TPUs are known for their higher strength, abrasion resistance, and thermal stability, making them ideal for applications in automotive and industrial sectors. In contrast, polyether-based TPUs offer better flexibility, hydrolytic stability, and resistance to harsh chemical environments, making them suitable for soft goods and footwear. Each type caters to specific end-use requirements, influencing their demand across various industries.</p></p>
