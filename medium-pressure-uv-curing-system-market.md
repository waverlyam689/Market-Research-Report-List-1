<p><ul><li>Spot Cure</li><li>Flood Cure</li><li>Focused Beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Medium Pressure UV curing systems are categorized into three primary market types: Spot Cure, Flood Cure, and Focused Beam. Spot Cure systems deliver concentrated UV light to specific areas, ideal for precise applications like bonding or coating small parts. Flood Cure systems emit broad UV radiation, curing large surfaces uniformly, making them suitable for high-speed production lines. Focused Beam systems combine aspects of both, directing UV light over a defined area with high intensity, allowing for effective curing in diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
