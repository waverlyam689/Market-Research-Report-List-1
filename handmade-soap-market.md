<p><ul><li>CP (Cold Process)</li><li>HP (Hot Process)</li><li>MP (Melt & Pour)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handmade soap market is characterized by three main types: Cold Process (CP), Hot Process (HP), and Melt & Pour (MP). CP involves mixing oils and lye at room temperature, allowing for creative designs and natural ingredients. HP entails cooking the soap mixture, speeding up saponification and yielding a more rustic texture. MP uses pre-made soap bases melted and poured into molds, making it beginner-friendly and versatile for customizations. Each type caters to different consumer preferences and skill levels.</p></p>
