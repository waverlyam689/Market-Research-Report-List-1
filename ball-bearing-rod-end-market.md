<p><p>The ball bearing rod end market features two primary types: internal threads and external threads. Internal thread rod ends have a threaded hole that allows for direct attachment to a rod or bolt, providing secure connections in applications requiring compact designs. In contrast, external thread rod ends have threads on the outside, facilitating easy mounting to surfaces or other components. Both types are crucial in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing, ensuring smooth movement and stability in machinery and structures.</p></p>
