<p><p>The trowels market is primarily divided into two categories: walk-behind trowels and ride-on trowels. Walk-behind trowels are compact and designed for smaller areas, offering maneuverability and ease of use in tight spaces. They are typically operated by a single operator. In contrast, ride-on trowels are larger, designed for extensive applications, and allow operators to sit while controlling the machine, which enhances efficiency and reduces operator fatigue. Both types are essential for finishing and smoothing concrete surfaces in construction projects.</p></p>
