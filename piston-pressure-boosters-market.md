<p><ul><li>Single Stage</li><li>Double Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piston pressure boosters market is primarily categorized into single-stage and double-stage types. Single-stage boosters utilize one piston to amplify pressure, offering a simpler design suitable for low to moderate pressure applications. In contrast, double-stage boosters employ two pistons in series, allowing for higher pressure outputs and improved efficiency in demanding applications. Each type serves specific needs, with single-stage models being more compact and cost-effective, whereas double-stage models are preferred for applications requiring higher pressures and performance.</p></p>
