<p><p>Cell-based label-free monitoring encompasses various technologies that enable real-time observation of cellular behaviors without labeling cells. Electrochemical methods detect changes in electrical signals due to cell interactions, offering sensitive measurements of cellular activities. Optical techniques utilize light to monitor cell responses, relying on changes in optical properties for analysis. Piezoelectric methods measure mass changes on sensor surfaces as cells adhere or detach, providing insights into cell dynamics. Each type leverages distinct physical principles to enhance cell monitoring capabilities, catering to diverse research needs.</p></p>
