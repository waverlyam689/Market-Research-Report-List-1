<p><p>The rear axle market includes three main types: Drive, Dead, and Lift axles. **Drive axles** are powered by the vehicle's engine, transmitting torque to the wheels for propulsion. **Dead axles** are non-powered, supporting the vehicle's weight without driving the wheels, often used in trailers. **Lift axles** can be raised or lowered to adjust load distribution, improving fuel efficiency when not needed. Each type serves specific purposes in vehicle design, balancing performance, load capacity, and efficiency.</p></p>
