<p><p>The whiteboard market consists of three primary types: melamine, porcelain, and glass. Melamine whiteboards are affordable and suitable for light use, but they wear out quickly. Porcelain whiteboards are durable and resistant to stains, making them ideal for frequent usage in professional environments. Glass whiteboards offer a sleek aesthetic, are easy to clean, and do not ghost, but they come at a higher price point. Each type caters to different user needs based on budget, usage, and design preferences.</p></p>
