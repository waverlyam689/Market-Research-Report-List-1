<p><p>The Pressurized Metered Dose Inhaler (pMDI) market consists of two primary types: Manual and Smart pMDIs. Manual pMDIs require users to coordinate their breathing with the actuation of the inhaler, offering basic functionality for medication delivery. In contrast, Smart pMDIs incorporate advanced technology, such as sensors and connectivity features, enabling real-time monitoring of medication usage and adherence. This technology can enhance patient engagement and optimize treatment outcomes by providing data insights to both patients and healthcare providers.</p></p>
