<p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM), a high-performance engineering plastic, is known for its excellent mechanical properties and dimensional stability. The market is segmented into two main types: homo-polyoxymethylene and co-polyoxymethylene. Homo-polyoxymethylene consists of a single type of polymer chain, providing superior strength and rigidity, making it suitable for automotive and industrial applications. Co-polyoxymethylene, which combines two different polymer chains, offers enhanced flexibility and impact resistance, catering to diverse applications in consumer goods and electronics, thus addressing varying performance needs.</p></p>
