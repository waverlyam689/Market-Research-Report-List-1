<p><p>The electrostatic discharges safe plastics market is categorized into two main types: ionic and non-ionic. Ionic type plastics have conductive additives that allow for the dissipation of static electricity, making them ideal for applications in electronic packaging and sensitive device manufacturing. Non-ionic type plastics, on the other hand, are designed to resist static electricity buildup without needing additives, making them suitable for environments where static control is critical but conductivity is not desired. Both types enhance safety in various industrial applications.</p></p>
