<p><p>The Medical Injection Molding Service market comprises two main types: thermoplastics and non-thermoplastics. Thermoplastics are versatile materials that can be repeatedly melted and reshaped, making them ideal for a variety of medical devices and components due to their durability and high precision. Non-thermoplastics, such as thermosets, undergo a chemical change when cured, offering excellent strength and heat resistance, which are vital for applications requiring stability and reliability. Both types cater to diverse medical applications, ensuring optimized performance and safety.</p></p>
