<p><p>The market for converting plastic to oil encompasses three primary processes: </p><p>1. **Pyrolysis Process:** Involves heating plastics in an oxygen-free environment, breaking them down into oil, gas, and char. </p><p>2. **Gasification and Synthesis Process:** Converts plastic into syngas through high-temperature reactions, which can be further transformed into liquid fuels. </p><p>3. **Catalytic Depolymerization Process:** Utilizes catalysts to lower the temperature required to break down polymers into oil, enhancing efficiency and yield. </p><p>These methods offer sustainable alternatives for plastic waste management.</p></p>
