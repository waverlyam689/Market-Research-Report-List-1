<p><p>The COF (Chip On Film) substrate market is categorized into two main types: single layer and double layer. Single layer COF substrates consist of a single film layer that provides a compact and lightweight solution for attaching microchips, ideal for applications requiring low profile designs. In contrast, double layer COF substrates incorporate an additional film layer, enhancing thermal performance and electrical insulation, making them suitable for more demanding applications that require improved reliability and efficiency in chip interconnections.</p></p>
