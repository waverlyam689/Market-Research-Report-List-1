Emulsion-type SB binders are produced through water-based processes, offering advantages like lower volatile organic compound emissions and easy application in various industries, including textiles and adhesives. Solution-type SB binders are solvent-based, known for their enhanced performance characteristics, high adhesion, and durability, making them ideal for applications in coatings and adhesives. Both types cater to diverse industry needs, emphasizing sustainability and performance.</p></p>
