<p><strong>The Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct TPMS</li><li>Indirect TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) market comprises two main types: Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. Direct TPMS uses sensors inside each tire to measure real-time pressure and transmit data to the vehicle's system, offering precise readings. Indirect TPMS relies on wheel speed sensors to estimate pressure based on tire rotation differences, making it less accurate but cost-effective. Both systems enhance safety by alerting drivers to underinflated tires, with Direct TPMS being more advanced and Indirect TPMS being simpler and cheaper.</p></p>
