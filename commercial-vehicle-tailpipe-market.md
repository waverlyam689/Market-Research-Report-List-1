<p><p>The commercial vehicle tailpipe market includes two main types: single tailpipe and double tailpipes. Single tailpipe systems are simpler, cost-effective, and commonly used in smaller or medium-duty vehicles. They focus on basic exhaust functionality. Double tailpipes, often seen in heavy-duty or high-performance vehicles, enhance exhaust flow, reduce backpressure, and improve engine efficiency. They also offer a more robust and aesthetic design. Both types cater to varying vehicle requirements, balancing performance, cost, and regulatory compliance for emissions control in commercial applications.</p></p>
