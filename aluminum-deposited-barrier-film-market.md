<p><p>The Aluminum Deposited Barrier Film market consists of three main types: VM-PET (Vacuum Metalized Polyethylene Terephthalate), VM-CPP (Vacuum Metalized Cast Polypropylene), and VM-OPP (Vacuum Metalized Oriented Polypropylene). VM-PET films are known for their high clarity and gas barrier properties, making them suitable for food packaging. VM-CPP offers excellent heat resistance and seals well, catering to both food and industrial applications. VM-OPP films provide a combination of flexibility and strength, commonly used in snack foods and confectionery packaging.</p></p>
