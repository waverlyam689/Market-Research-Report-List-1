<p><p>The submarine fiber optics market primarily comprises two types: submarine communications cables and submarine optical cables. Submarine optical cables, on the other hand, are specialized high-capacity cables that utilize light signals for data transfer, ensuring high bandwidth and minimal signal loss over long distances.
