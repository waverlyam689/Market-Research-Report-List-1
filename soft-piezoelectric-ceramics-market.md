<p><p>The soft piezoelectric ceramics market primarily consists of materials like PZT5A and PZT5H, which are lead zirconate titanate compositions known for their high piezoelectric response. PZT5A exhibits enhanced sensitivity and flexibility, making it suitable for applications requiring high deformation levels, such as sensors and actuators. Conversely, PZT5H offers a greater degree of temperature stability and fatigue resistance, making it ideal for harsher environments. Both types are widely utilized in industrial, automotive, and consumer electronic applications to convert mechanical energy into electrical signals.</p></p>
