<p><strong>The Steel Industry Refractory Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brick Refractories</li><li>Monolithic Refractories</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steel industry refractory materials market is primarily divided into two types: brick refractories and monolithic refractories. Brick refractories are preformed shapes, often used in high-temperature applications like blast furnaces and ladles, providing durability and thermal resistance. Monolithic refractories, on the other hand, consist of unshaped materials like castables and gunning mixes, offering flexibility in installation and adaptability to complex geometries. Both types are essential for maintaining the integrity of steel production processes under extreme conditions.</p></p>
