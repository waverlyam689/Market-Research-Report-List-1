<p><strong>The Inkjet Heads Ejection Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Inkjet Heads</li><li>Drop on Demand Inkjet Heads</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Inkjet head ejection technology primarily comprises two market types: Continuous Inkjet Heads and Drop-on-Demand Inkjet Heads. Continuous Inkjet Heads function by maintaining a constant flow of ink, which is charged and directed to print on surfaces, ideal for high-speed printing applications. In contrast, Drop-on-Demand Inkjet Heads release ink droplets only when required, allowing for greater control and flexibility, making them suitable for various printing materials and lower-volume applications. Each technology serves distinct industrial and commercial needs effectively.</p></p>
