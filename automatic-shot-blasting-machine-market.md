<p><p>The automatic shot blasting machine market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Hanger Type**: Utilizes overhead tracks to hang components, facilitating efficient blasting of larger parts. </p><p>2. **Tumblast Machine**: Involves components being placed in a chamber, where they tumble with abrasive media for thorough cleaning.  </p><p>3. **Continuous Through-feed**: Allows parts to pass through the equipment for constant blasting, maximizing efficiency.  </p><p>4. **Rotary Table**: Features a rotating table where parts are blasted while stationary, ensuring uniform coverage.  </p><p>5. **Others**: This includes specialized or less common types that cater to unique industrial needs.</p></p>
