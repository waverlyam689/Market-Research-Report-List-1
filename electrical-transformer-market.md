<p><strong>The Electrical Transformer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Step Up Transformer & Step Down Transformer</li><li>Three Phase Transformer & Single Phase Transformer</li><li>Two Winding Transformer & Auto Transformer</li><li>Outdoor Transformer & Indoor Transformer</li><li>Oil Cooled & Dry Type Transformer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrical transformer market comprises several types: Step Up Transformers increase voltage for transmission, while Step Down Transformers reduce voltage for distribution. Three Phase Transformers support three-phase systems, whereas Single Phase Transformers are used for simpler applications. Two Winding Transformers have distinct primary and secondary windings, while Auto Transformers feature a shared winding. Outdoor Transformers are designed for external installation, compared to Indoor Transformers which are suited for enclosed spaces. Oil Cooled Transformers utilize oil for cooling, while Dry Type Transformers rely on air.</p></p>
