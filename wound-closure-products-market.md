<p><p>The wound closure products market encompasses various techniques for securing wounds. Sutures are threads used to stitch tissues together, while surgical staples are metal fasteners that quickly close incisions. Wound closure strips, typically adhesive strips, secure skin edges without penetrating. Adhesives and tissue sealants are liquid solutions that bond tissues and offer a flexible closure. Hemostats are agents that help control bleeding during surgery. Each type plays a crucial role in efficient wound management and healing.</p></p>
