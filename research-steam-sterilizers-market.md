<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Research Steam Sterilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Cycle</li><li>SFPP Cycle</li><li>Pre-Vac Cycle</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Research steam sterilizers are essential for ensuring the sterility of medical and laboratory instruments. The market features various types: </p><p>1. **Gravity Cycle**: Uses gravity to displace air and allow steam penetration, making it suitable for non-porous items.</p><p>2. **SFPP Cycle (Steam Flush Pressure Pulse)**: Employs rapid steam pulses to eliminate air pockets, enhancing penetration for porous loads.</p><p>3. **Pre-Vac Cycle (Pre-Vacuum)**: Creates a vacuum to remove air before steam injection, ensuring thorough sterilization for complex instruments. </p><p>Each cycle addresses specific sterilization needs, influencing market demand and applications.</p></p>
